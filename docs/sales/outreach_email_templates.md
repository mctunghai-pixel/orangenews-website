# Outreach Email Templates — Phase 9.2

> **Phase 9.2 deliverable.** 4 cold-email variants tuned to specific customer segments + 1 warm-network variant. Each in **English + Mongolian Cyrillic**. Variables in `{curly braces}` for the founder to fill per send. `[FOUNDER ADJUST]` tags mark voice/tone passes that need founder's cultural-register judgment.

## Operational notes (founder reminder)

- **Don't blast:** send 3-5 cold emails per day MAX. Higher-conversion bespoke beats high-volume mass send.
- **Track responses** in a spreadsheet — sent date, response, follow-up date. The Phase 9.2.1 deliverable can be a CRM-lite tracker if needed; for now a Google Sheet is sufficient.
- **Follow up once** if no response after 5 business days. Single short follow-up only — don't become noise.
- **Pre-flight every send:** verify the live site is healthy (front page loads, video feed renders) within 5 min of clicking Send. A broken demo at the moment of click is the worst outcome.
- **Voice register check:** [FOUNDER ADJUST] — review the Mongolian copy for cultural-register correctness before first send. Engineer drafted the above in respectful-formal Cyrillic; founder may want softer/sharper depending on relationship to recipient.
