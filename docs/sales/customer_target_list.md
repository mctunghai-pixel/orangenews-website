## Outreach prioritization (engineer recommendation)

Sequence the first 3 outreach attempts to maximize founder-confidence-per-meeting:

1. **Capitron Bank** (Tier 1, smaller decision body) — fast-close test of the pitch
2. **BDSec** (Tier 2, research-business native) — second easiest "they understand the value" close
3. **TDB Bank** (Tier 1 anchor) — armed with insights from #1 and #2

Save the regulator + media tier for after the first closed pilot — having a paying customer makes the strategic-partnership pitches credible.
